Emotional Intelligence

It is encouraging to read that emotional intelligence can be trained and developed, even as adults. By being aware of how to perceive emotion in our bodies' through reactions, we can learn a lot about ourselves. A high-resolution perception, or the ability to being able to identify emotions rising in our bodies, is a useful tool for any situation. I am interested in developing this further in myself, and looking at different solutions for managing emotions.
I achieved the two minutes of mindfulness by sitting on the beach and letting myself be blown about in the wind. It certainly took my mind off other things, but I think I'll aim for somewhere a little calmer next time.
